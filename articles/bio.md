## Research

**Geometric AI Theory** — A mathematical framework modeling reasoning as geodesic search on Riemannian manifolds. Published as a book series (2026):

- *Geometric Ethics: The Mathematical Structure of Moral Reasoning* (v1.24, 30 chapters)
- *Geometric Reasoning: From Search to Manifolds* (in progress, 16 chapters)
- *Geometric Cognition*, *Geometric Economics*, *Geometric Law*, *Geometric Medicine*, *Geometric Communication* (planned/in progress)

**Theory Radar** — Symbolic formula search engine for interpretable binary classification. GPU-accelerated beam search with PLS projections, meta-learned pruning, and TurboQuant-compressed beam storage. Paper under review at IEEE Transactions on Artificial Intelligence.

**Measuring Progress Toward AGI** — Kaggle competition across five cognitive tracks, applying geometric methods including symmetry augmentation, SPD manifolds, Poincare ball embeddings, and topological data analysis.
